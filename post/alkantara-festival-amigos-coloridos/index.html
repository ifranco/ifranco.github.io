Alkantara Festival - Amigos Coloridos

May 22, 2010 1 min read

Alkantara is one of the reference Portuguese festivals in performance arts, happening in both Lisbon and Porto.

I’ll be showing a new performance with Lígia on the 24th of May, in Teatro S. Luís (Lisbon), on “Amigos Coloridos”. It’s kind of a surprise show, with intensive blind dates between performers and the audience.

“ Amigos Coloridos@Alkantara ”

The whole festival will go on until the 9th of June, so be sure to check the rest of the program.

http://www.alkantarafestival.pt/
