Projects

All Work Art

Haptic Field

Haptic Field is a participatory multi-sensory installation merging wearable technology, contemporary fashion, and an exploration of the senses beyond sight.

Prynth

Prynth are programmable sound synthesizers powered by single-board computers. They combine the advantages of dedicated hardware with the flexibility of programming.

The Mitt

“The Mitt” is a digital synthesizer that explores the fine motor skills of the human hand and the performative aspects of micro gestures.

Vibropixels

Wearable tactile actuators.

Ilinx

Ilinx is an immersive installation where the visitors wear haptic feedback garments.

Strings of Thought 2

Strings of Thought 2 is an interactive installation that invites interaction with a performer through a wearable meta-instrument to create a collaborative soundscape.

Contact

Contact is both an interactive installation and a performance of electronic music. The set is composed of several instruments that are first played by Ivan Franco and later offered to the audience, inviting participation and collaboration in the musical event.

Strings of Thought

Strings of Thought is a contemporary dance piece where the dancer controls light and sound through a set of suspended cables.

Ynvisible

A YDreams spin-off dedicated to printed electronics and electrochromic displays.

I-Garment

An intelligent firefighter garment, with embedded sensors and realtime data relay.

Algum Dia Tinha de Ser a Sério

Algum Dia Tinha De Ser A Sério is a dance piece about the relationship between a woman and the imaginary companion of her dreams.

Audience Entertainment

A YDreams spin-off dedicated to large-scale multi-user interaction in cinemas and events.

Airstick

A musical controllers played “in the air” like a bi-dimensional Theremin.

Augmented Reality

A showcase of YDreams AR technologies.

Brand and Retail

YDreams brand and retail experiences.

Museums

YDreams design and implementation of turn-key interactive solutions for museums.
